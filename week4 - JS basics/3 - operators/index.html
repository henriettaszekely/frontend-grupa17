JavaScript operators

    1. Arithmetic:
  • Addition: +
  • Subtraction: -
  • Multiplication: *
  • Division: /
  • Modulus: %
    2. Comparison:
  • Equal: == & ===
  • Not equal: != & !==
  • Greater than: >
  • Greater than or equal to: >=
  • Less then: <
  • Less than or equal to: <=
    3. Logical:
  • And: &&
  • Or: ||
  • Not: !
    4. Unary:
  • Negation: -
  • Increment: ++
  • Decrement: --
  • typeof
    5. Ternary:
  • condition operator: condition ? exprIfTrue : exprIfFalse
